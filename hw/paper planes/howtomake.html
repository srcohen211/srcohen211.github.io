A The best paper plane in 5 steps

When I was a child I loved making paper planes, this is an Ode to my child hood

Tools Directions

Tools

  • Both of your hands
  • Single piece of paper

Directions

  1. Rip out sheet of paper
  2. Fold corners into the middle
  3. Fold page in half
  4. Fold sides and create your wings
  5. Enjoy flying your plane!
